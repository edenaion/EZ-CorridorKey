# Video Direction Prompts

Direction for video content associated with ez-CorridorKey.

---

## Demo/Showcase Videos

### Style Direction
- **Color grade:** Warm dark. Lift shadows slightly with an olive/amber cast. Highlights clipped to warm white.
- **Motion:** Smooth, deliberate camera movements. No shaky cam. Slow push-ins on results.
- **Pacing:** Let the results breathe. 2-3 second holds on before/after comparisons.
- **Transitions:** Hard cuts or simple crossfades. No flashy transitions. The VFX results ARE the spectacle.
- **Lower thirds:** Yellow `#FFF203` text on semi-transparent dark bars. ALL CAPS. Open Sans Bold.

### Music Mood
- Electronic ambient with subtle tension
- Builds during processing, resolves when results appear
- Think: Corridor Crew's tech breakdown music — confident, forward-moving, not overhyped
- Avoid: Corporate stock music, dubstep drops, acoustic guitar

### Text Animation
- Snap-on appearance (0-2 frame fade)
- Wide letter-spacing tracking in from center
- Yellow accent underlines that wipe on from left
- No bouncy, springy, or playful motion

---

## Tutorial / Walkthrough Videos

### Screen Recording Style
- Dark OS theme / dark IDE theme
- Terminal with dark background, yellow prompt accent
- Mouse cursor visible, deliberate movements
- Highlight important UI elements with yellow `#FFF203` rectangle outline (2px, no radius)
- Zoom to 150-200% for detail shots of settings panels

### Voiceover Tone
- Casual authority — "Here's what we're doing" not "In this tutorial we will explore"
- First person — "I'm going to run inference on these clips"
- Technical when needed — don't avoid terms like "sRGB", "premultiplied alpha"
- Honest about limitations — "This needs 24GB VRAM, so you'll want a 3090 or better"

## Social Clips (Short-form)

### Format
- 9:16 vertical or 1:1 square
- 15-60 seconds
- Hook in first 2 seconds: dramatic before/after wipe

### Composition
- Full-screen before/after wipe (vertical split moving left to right)
- Green screen on left, clean key on right
- Yellow accent line at the split point
- Bold text overlay: "AI KEYING" or "ONE CLICK" in ALL CAPS yellow
- End card: Diamond mark + "CorridorKey" + GitHub link
