# Aesthetic Keywords

Categorized keyword library for ez-CorridorKey brand-consistent content generation.

---

## Visual Style

### Adjectives
cinematic, industrial, angular, bold, technical, professional, sharp, geometric, atmospheric, dark, warm-dark, high-contrast, matte, flat, clean, minimal, deliberate, confident

### Textures
matte finish, brushed metal, carbon fiber, dark grain, subtle noise, atmospheric haze, film grain, dark steel, warm shadows

### Lighting
low-key, dramatic side-light, warm ambient, editing suite glow, monitor light in darkness, rim light on dark, warm practical lighting, atmospheric haze with backlight

### Composition
centered subject, rule-of-thirds for UI, generous negative space, horizontal split for before/after, tight crops on detail, pulled-back context shots in dark environments

---

## Mood / Atmosphere

### Emotions
confident, authoritative, impressive, precise, satisfying (the "clean key" feeling), professional pride, craft mastery, technical awe

### Energy
controlled intensity, steady confidence, methodical precision, quiet power (the tool works and the results speak for themselves)

### Environment
dark editing suite, VFX studio, post-production facility, multiple monitors showing footage, controlled lighting, professional workspace

---

## Technical Modifiers

### Camera Angles
straight-on (UI screenshots), slight overhead (workspace shots), eye-level (product hero), macro (detail shots of hair/edge quality)

### Rendering Style
flat/vector for icons and UI elements, photorealistic for hero imagery, dark cinematic for atmospheric shots

### Color Grading
warm shadows (olive/amber lift), neutral midtones, clean whites, yellow accent pops, avoid blue/teal color grades, avoid desaturated looks

---

## Anti-Keywords (DO NOT USE)

### Styles That Don't Fit
- cute, playful, whimsical, bubbly, cheerful
- pastel, light, airy, soft, gentle
- corporate, enterprise, formal, stiff
- retro, vintage, nostalgic (unless VFX history context)
- neon, cyberpunk, vaporwave (too stylized)

### Overused Cliches
- stock photo people pointing at screens
- generic "AI brain" visualizations
- matrix-style green text
- lens flare abuse
- gradient mesh backgrounds

### Brand Conflicts
- green as a decorative/brand color (it's the adversary)
- rounded/pill-shaped UI elements
- light mode / white backgrounds
- serif fonts in UI
- blue-black backgrounds (too cold — use warm near-black)
- pure `#000000` black (too stark — use `#0E0D00` or `#141300`)
